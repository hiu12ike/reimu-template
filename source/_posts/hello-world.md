The spring anime season is here! I didn’t care too much about last season, but this one I actually care about. Let’s go over what I will engage with this season. You’ll notice a lot of this I have read before or am going to read.

The anime I will 100% watch start to finish.

## What I will give a shot

### My ridiculous reincarnation

The premise of this alone was so funny I was already going to give it a shot. Then I watched episode 1. And I was just blown away. It was so insanely funny I was **NOT** ready. Between the infinite rib army to the rib selfcest yaoi, it went all over the place. I was hooked on the absurdity of it all. Theres 2 reasons this isn’t in must-watch: The protagonist, and the potential of the show. The protagonist is a very lewd guy, and I think many people might find him annoying. The FMC’s cold demeanor will work for many, but the male protag will not work for as many. I mean, his whole body is quite literally an erogenous zone, which will probably turn people away as well as his general raunchyness. And even if it works at the beginning, there’s no guarantee it’ll work for the whole show, tying into the second reason. I don’t know how long the show can keep its comedy for.. Thankfully, it’s short and only has 12 episodes, but it’ll have to be seen if it can keep being funny, and have its lewd protagonist, for the entire runtime. The different animation styles and live-action in the OP seem so interesting.
![screenshot](https://cdn.imgchest.com/files/0f9ba1b092c0.jpg)

### Magical Sisters LuluttoLilly

Studio Pierrot does a mahou shoujo for the first time since 1998. Does it look good? Eh. I didn’t get too hooked on episode 1. However, it is kinda refreshing seeing a magical girl get powers and not have to fight evils. I’m interested to see where it goes, and the OP and ED are cute(except for ILLIT singing the OP), but I’m not too interested in the current state so far. Let’s see where it goes. Fansub only.
